The Walking Dead

Genre: Series, Action, Drama, Horror

Country: USA

Year: 2010

Episode: 11

Synopsis *The Walking Dead* follows a group of survivors in a post-apocalyptic world where a zombie outbreak has turned most of the population into bloodthirsty creatures. Initially, the story centers around Rick Grimes, a former sheriff who wakes up from a coma to find the world has completely changed. He quickly reunites with his family and joins other survivors, leading them through harsh survival challenges.

In addition to facing the undead, the group must confront even greater threats from other groups of people who have become brutal and ruthless. Throughout the series, viewers witness the characters' psychological changes, moral dilemmas, and struggles to retain their humanity in dire circumstances.
