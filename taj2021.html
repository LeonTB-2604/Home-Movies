Tom and Jerry (2021)

Info

Genre: Movies, Comedy

Country: USA

Year: 2021

Duration: 101 minutes

Synopsis: The film revolves around the ongoing rivalry between Tom, the cat, and Jerry, the mouse. When Kayla, an event planner, needs a cat to catch Jerry at a high-end hotel in New York, she chooses Tom to help her deal with the chaos Jerry is causing.

As the battle between Tom and Jerry escalates, chaos ensues. Kayla must find a way to mediate between the two characters while also handling a larger plot unfolding at the hotel.
