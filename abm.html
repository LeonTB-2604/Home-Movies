Angry Birds Movie

Info

Genre: Movies, Comedy

Country: USA

Year: 2016

Duration: 97 minutes

Synopsis: The plot is set on Bird Island, where flightless birds live peacefully. Red, who struggles with his temper, is sent to anger management classes after an outburst. There, he meets Chuck (a fast-talking yellow bird) and Bomb (a bird that literally explodes when upset). When green pigs, led by Leonard, arrive on the island, they initially appear to be friendly, but they later steal the birds' eggs. Red, along with his new friends, embarks on a mission to reclaim the stolen eggs from Piggy Island, culminating in a battle.
