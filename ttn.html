Titanic

Info

Genre: Movie, Romance

Country: USA

Year: 2012

Duration: 194 minutes

Synopsis: The film tells the fictionalized story of Rose DeWitt Bukater, a young wealthy American woman, and Jack Dawson, a poor artist, who fall in love aboard the RMS Titanic during its ill-fated maiden voyage.
As the Titanic encounters an iceberg and begins to sink, Jack and Rose must fight for their survival amidst the chaos and panic. The film explores themes of class, social inequality, and the power of love in the face of tragedy.
